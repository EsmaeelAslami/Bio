About Me

Biomedical Engineer • AI Researcher • Innovator at the Intersection of Neuroscience and Technology

Biography

I am a dedicated Biomedical Engineering student at Islamic Azad University, passionate about bridging the gap between medical science and advanced technology. My academic journey is driven by a deep curiosity about how engineering principles can be applied to solve complex healthcare challenges.

My research focuses on medical imaging, brain-computer interfaces (BCI), and the development of intelligent systems that can enhance diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation processes.

With hands-on laboratory experience and publications in control systems and bioinstrumentation, I've developed a strong foundation in both theoretical concepts and practical applications. My work spans from developing deep learning algorithms for medical image analysis to designing experimental setups for BCI research.

What excites me most is the potential for technology to transform healthcare delivery, making it more accessible, accurate, and personalized. I believe that the future of medicine lies at the intersection of biological understanding and technological innovation.
